The Furry Con

I went to my first furry convention in Columbus shortly before the end of the school year. I met a lot of new people and had the best time. I will return next year.

Fourth of July

As is tradition in my family, we all went up to my grandparents cottage in Michigan for a week to enjoy the firework display that the rich people set off. There weren't many fireworks that I saw, but we still had a lot of fun on the water.

Computer Coma

One night, I was playing Sea Of Thieves with some friends when my computer decided to do what I lovingly call the "Syrup freeze", named for my Discord name and the fact that I'm the only one who experiences a periodic 15-30s complete freeze of the game for no discernible reason. This particular freeze took about 1 minute to resolve, and did so by crashing my computer completely. When it rebooted, it would not leave the advanced startup options menu built into Windows, and I was eventually forced to wipe most of my data after 3 days of non-stop struggling with it.

Conclusion

This summer was a welcome break from the monotony of schoolwork. It gave me exactly the amount of time I needed to prepare for the final year of school.
